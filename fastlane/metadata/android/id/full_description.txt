A modern and expressive Material 3 YouTube Music client for Android, designed for performance and extensibility.

Features:

- Play any song or video from YouTube Music
- Background playback
- Personalized quick picks
- Library management
- Download and save songs for offline playback
- Search songs, albums, artists, videos, and playlists
- Real-time synced lyrics
- Login support with YouTube Music account
- Sync songs, artists, albums, and playlists with your account
- Skip silence
- Import playlists
- Audio normalization
- Adjust tempo and pitch
- Local playlist management
- Reorder songs in playlist or queue
- Light, Dark, Black, and Dynamic themes
- Sleep timer
- Material 3 design
- Custom Discord Rich Presence
- And more...
